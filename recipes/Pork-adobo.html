Filipino Pork Adobo

This recipe is submitted by Joy from allrecipe.com.

Adobo is a filipino dish that is cooked with vinegar and soysauce. It is perfect for all occasion.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup distilled white vinegar
  • 1 cup soy sauce
  • ½ cup ketchup
  • 1 tablespoon minced garlic
  • 3 bay leaves
  • 1 teaspoon fresh-ground black pepper
  • 2 ½ pounds lean pork, cut into 1 inch cubes
  • 1 pound small green beans, trimmed (optional)

Directions

  1. Step 1

    Stir together the vinegar, soy sauce, ketchup, garlic, and bay leaves in a large saucepan.

  2. Step 2

    Add the cubed pork, and bring to a boil over high heat.

  3. Step 3

    Reduce heat to medium-low, cover, and simmer until the pork is tender, about 2 ½ hours.

  4. Step 4

    Stir occasionally. If using the green beans, add them during the last half hour of cooking.

  5. Step 5

    Transfer the Pork Adobo to a serving dish. Serve with hot steaming rice.
